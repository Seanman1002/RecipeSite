Eggs Benedict

Here is one of my favorite Breakfast recipes. A classic Eggs Benedict

Ingredients

  • 6 Jumbo sized eggs
  • 1/4 Cup unsalted butter
  • 4 slices of canadian bacon
  • Whole Wheat English Muffins
  • splash of lemon juice
  • Seasoning(E.G. salt, pepper, dill weed, old bay seasoning, parsley flakes)
  • Tablespoon of distilled white vinegar

Directions

  1. Boil a pot of water with the tablespoon white vinegar
  2. Once the water is boiling, crack open 4 of the Eggs into the pot, cover on medium heat for 8-12 minutes
  3. Fry the canadian bacon in a pan with some oil
  4. once the canadian bacon is cooked, put it on a plate
  5. Once the eggs are cooked, take off the pot from the heat
  6. Melt the unsalted butter in a bowl, mix with the lemon juice and some pepper, toss into a small saucepan or pot then use the 2 remaining eggs by separating the yolks to mix into the mixture
  7. Put the pot with the mixture on low heat and mix without scrambling the yolks for 1-3 minutes.
  8. Put the English muffins on a plate put 1 egg on each muffin, season as you like for the eggs, top with the bacon and sauce mixture.
  9. Enjoy and celebrate making a great breakfast :D
