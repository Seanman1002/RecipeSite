Banana Stuffed French Toast

Here we have a rather delectably sweet breakfast recipe! I bet you are wondering 'Sean, how is this even Diabetic friendly!?' Well I am glad you asked voice in my head! We will make some diabetic friendly substitutions!

Ingredients

  • Ground Nutmeg
  • Lemon Juice
  • Allspice
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Neufchatel Cream cheese
  • Raison Bread
  • Bananas
  • Canola Spray
  • Powdered Stevia
  • Vanilla Extract
  • 8 Egg Whites and 4 Egg Yolks

Directions

  1. Soften the Neufchatel Cream Cheese then put in a large mixing bowl
  2. Add some ground nutmeg, cinnamon, Allspice, and a splash of lemon juice to the mixing bowl, stir till thoroughly mixed together
  3. Slice up at least 4 Bananas into little slices and add to the mixing bowl as well then stir it again
  4. In a separate mixing bowl, Crack open and separate 8 Egg whites and crack another 4 eggs into the bowl, Add a 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  5. Whisk the Egg mixture
  6. In a separate small bowl, add 1 Cup of powdered Stevia
  7. Fire up the stovetop to medium heat and spray some canola spray on a griddle pan
  8. Take one slice of Raison bread, dip into the egg mixture throw some stevia on one side then lay the sugar side down onto the griddle. add a scoop of banana mixture on the bread then grab a second slice and do the same then put it on top of the mixture
  9. Brown both sides of the french toast then serve onto a plate, rinse and repeat
  10. Voila! you have a sweet breakfast! feel free to top with berries if you want to
